USA · Olympic Gold
XANDER SCHAUFFELE
PGA Tour · #3 World Ranking

One of the most complete ball-strikers in the modern game. Schauffele's strokes-gained approach profile is among the elite — combining tight dispersion off the tee with a surgical iron game that consistently converts birdie looks at a rate few players can match.

Around the green, Schauffele demonstrates elite touch and trajectory control. His flat-stick performance has improved meaningfully year-over-year. The combination of long-game dominance and a sharpened short game creates a player who can win on any course setup.
